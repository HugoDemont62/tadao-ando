Tadao Ando

Concrete

is the

masterfully simple, eternally

timeless,

signature

philosophy of Ando.

We bring his vision to life, from concept to completion.

"I believe that the way people live can be directed a little by architecture. When I design, I think of the atmosphere that will touch the spirit of people who live there. The way of life will be influenced by the place. In all my works, light is an important controlling factor. I create enclosed spaces mainly by means of thick concrete walls. The primary reason is to create a place for the individual, a zone for oneself within society. When the external factors of a city's environment require the wall to be without openings, the interior must be especially full and satisfying. But I have always preferred living spaces that also open up to the outside world. The fundamental problem is the intricate relationship between interior and exterior. I like to work with a void - the space contained within. The most important consideration is the positioning of the opening that connects these two worlds. The size, positioning, and shape of the opening must be determined with great care."
